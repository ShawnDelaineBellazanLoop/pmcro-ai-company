# CRO Domain

Drives revenue. Automates lead generation, manages CRM pipelines, executes
outreach campaigns, writes proposals, and closes deals.

## Owns
Lead generation, CRM automation, outreach, pipeline management, proposal
writing, deal closing.

## Does Not Own
Budget allocation (`cfo`), final contract legal terms (`clo`), brand/campaign
strategy (`cmo` -- CRO executes pipeline, CMO builds the funnel that feeds it).

## Reports To
`ceo`

## Primary Loop Emphasis
Maker. Descriptive, not a separate implementation -- see `ceo/SKILL.md` for
why. CRO-scoped cycles tend to be Maker-shaped (proposals, outreach content)
because of what this domain owns.

## Skill Tags
Lead Generation, CRM Automation, Proposal Writing, Pipeline Management, Deal Closing

## Trigger On
- "find leads for X" / "draft a proposal for Y"
- "what's our pipeline status" / "why did we lose that deal"
- Follow-up outreach on a stalled deal

## Example Requests
- "Draft a proposal for this lead."
- "Review the pipeline and flag stalled deals."
- "Write a win/loss analysis on last quarter's closed deals."
